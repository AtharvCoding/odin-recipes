Herb Watermelon Feta Salad

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you need to make Herb Watermelon Feta Salad:

  • Watermelon: ½ large chilled seedless watermelon, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • Red Onion: 1 small red onion, sliced
  • Fresh Basil: 1 cup thinly sliced fresh basil leaves
  • Fresh Cilantro: 1 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Fresh Mint: ½ cup minced fresh mint leaves
  • Lime Juice: Juice of 2 limes
  • Feta Cheese: 1 (4 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese
  • Olive Oil: 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar: 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, or more to taste
  • Salt and Black Pepper: To taste

How to Make Herb Watermelon Feta Salad

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here is a brief overview of what you can expect when you make this refreshing salad:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Gently toss watermelon, onion, basil, cilantro, mint, lime juice, feta cheese, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and black pepper together in a large bowl.
