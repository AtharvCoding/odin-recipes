Garlic Chicken Fried Chicken

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you need to make Garlic Chicken Fried Chicken:

  • Garlic: Fresh minced garlic adds deep flavor to the marinade.
  • Chicken Breasts: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts are pounded thin.
  • Buttermilk: Used to marinate the chicken and tenderize it.
  • All-purpose Flour: For coating the chicken before frying.
  • Baking Powder: Added to the flour mixture for a crispier crust.
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika add flavor.
  • Oil: For deep frying the chicken until golden brown.

How to make Garlic Chicken Fried Chicken

You’ll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here is a brief overview of what you can expect:

  1. Whisk buttermilk and garlic together, then marinate the chicken for several hours.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and seasonings in a shallow dish.
  3. Remove chicken from marinade and dredge in the flour mixture.
  4. Fry the chicken in hot oil until golden brown and fully cooked inside.
  5. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
